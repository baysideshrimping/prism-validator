File Naming Convention

{STATE}_{TYPE}_{SEASON}_{MONTH}.csv
GA_COVID_2025-26_Jul.csv Georgia COVID data for July
NY_FLU_2025-26_Oct.csv New York Flu data for October
CA_RSV_2024-25_Sept.csv California RSV data for September
  • STATE: 2-letter state abbreviation (GA, NY, CA, TX...)
  • TYPE: COVID, FLU, or RSV
  • SEASON: Fiscal year format YYYY-YY (2025-26)
  • MONTH: Jul, Aug, Sept, Oct, Nov, Dec, Jan, Feb, Mar, Apr, May, Jun
